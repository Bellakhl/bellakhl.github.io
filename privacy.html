Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

Rider Video Publisher ("the App") is a personal automated video upload tool.

Data Collected

  • YouTube credentials — OAuth tokens used solely to upload videos to your channel.
  • Video content — Esports highlight clips downloaded from public sources.
  • No personal information — No names, emails, or location data is collected.

Data Sharing

The App does not share or sell your data. All operations run locally on your device.

Data Retention

OAuth tokens and video files are stored locally. You can delete them anytime by removing the config file or clip directories.

Contact

Contact the developer via the email listed on the homepage.
